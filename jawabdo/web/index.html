How it works

Four steps.
Zero excuses.

01
📸
Snap & Pin
Broken road, dead streetlight, overflowing garbage — photograph it. GPS pins your exact location and auto-detects your municipal ward. 30 seconds, done.
02
👊
Community Backs You
Your issue hits the ward feed. Neighbours upvote, comment, share. Community backing isn't just moral support — it's the second key that unlocks escalation. Your outrage, made undeniable.
03
🔺
Auto-Escalation
Officials go silent AND the community is backing it? Jawab Do fires it up the chain — Ward → Municipal → State → Media. No follow-up calls. No begging.
04
📊
Public Report Card
Every ward graded every month. Every official scored publicly. Permanent, searchable, shareable. No place to hide when election season comes.
